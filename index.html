    About MedMinder

    MedMinder is the simple way you and your loved one can be sure to take meds on time, everyday. At MedMinder we do not sleep, your health and frequency of medicaion intake is of utmost importance to us.

    Using the latest in AI and blockchain technologies, we have created a 'set it and forget it' program to take the stress out of remembering to take and distribute medications!

    Features

    A feature rich program that allows you and your loved one the freedom of not having to worry about when to take medicaions.

    • Daily Reminders

      We don't skip a beat, and we don't expect you to either. Our reminders are pragmatically set for when you need them, and however many times you need them!

    • Easy Setup

      Not tech savy? Neither are our family members. We've taken the mystery out of our program and made it easy for ANYONE to use!

    • Find More Free Time

      Instead of needing to remember to take your medicines, let us remind you. Go about your day and wait for our call!

    • Choose Your Reminder

      Notifications, text reminders, or scheduled phone calls. The choice of how you revceive your reminder is totally up to you!
